HISTORY

Nancha’s Pizza is a bumiputera-owned company that opened in 2021. Nancha’s Pizza Restaurant serves and promises its own delicacies with the best pizza recipes. The combination of selected herbs is the main secret in the preparation of our pizza. Nancha’s Pizza provides delicious pizzas to suit the tastes of all children, teenagers, adults and the elderly. A wide selection of pizza flavors is available for Nancha’s Pizza fans to choose from.

Pizza lovers can enjoy pizza in our shop or can take-away. Nancha’s Pizza is located at Lot 2477, Jalan, Taman Komersil Senawang, 70450 Senawang, Negeri Sembilan. We start operating at 10am until 10pm, every day.

FOUNDER

Natasha Azeera Binti Aznan is the founder of Nancha’s Pizza. She has created her own unique pizza recipes and has great and delicious taste. She started a small-scale pizza business from home and made promotions on social media like Facebook and Instagram. After 5 months, Nancha’s Pizza started to get attention and became the choice of pizza fans and now she has managed to open her own pizza shop.

She aspires to further expand the business of Nancha’s Pizza and is working to open Nancha’s Pizza branches in other states in Malaysia.
